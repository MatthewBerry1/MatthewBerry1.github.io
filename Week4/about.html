About Us - Don't B Homeless

At Don't B Homeless, we believe in the transformative power of getting a home to call your own.
Founded in 2004 by a passionate estate agent. Matthew Berry,
our mission is to provide unforgettable home locations that go beyond the ordinary.

Our Story

Every journey has a beginning, and ours started on the bustling city of Dubai.
After a chance meeting between Matthew, John and Mike, three avid students from different corners of the world,
Don't B Homeless was born out of a shared dream to help others experience the beauty and wonder of calling a place your own.
